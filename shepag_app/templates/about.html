Who We Are

ShePAG, founded in January 2024, is a pioneering association of seven visionary Ghanaian women leaders. Specializing in exporting high-quality products globally, from organic shea nuts to innovative baby foods, we are a dynamic force in the international market. Our mission is to redefine success and empower women in Ghana's export industry, fostering innovation and conquering new horizons.

Mission

Empower and uplift Ghanaian women in the export sector, fostering collaboration, growth, and excellence.

Vision

To be a leading force in the global export industry, empowering and uplifting women across Ghana.

Our Strength

ShePAG combines diverse talents and expertise to excel in the export industry. Over 70 years combined experience in farming and agriculture to food science, entrepreneurship, project management and business administration, our team is poised to conquer international markets.

Core Values

Empowerment

Committed to empowering women in the export industry.

Collaboration

Fostering a culture of collaboration for collective success.

Excellence

Striving for excellence in every facet of our operations.

Innovation

Driving innovation to stay at the forefront of the export market.
